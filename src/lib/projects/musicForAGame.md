## Sound design

The sounds were made by Akhun, Troglody and me. I had to make many footsteps sounds. I went for an approach that lets me create many variations of a single footstep sound in no time. The idea was to use a footstep sample from freesound.org and to use it as a convolver. Then I created an impulse generator along with several tone-modifying effects and got many footstep sounds. The variations were made by randomizing some parameters everytime the impulse was played using bitwig's random module.
