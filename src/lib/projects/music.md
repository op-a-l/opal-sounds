You can listen to what I've made on soundcloud thouhg. I am quite proud of some songs like [_transients_](https://soundcloud.com/underscore-opal/traansients?si=91329f2a47674dc3930a27b80688b512&utm_source=clipboard&utm_medium=text&utm_campaign=social_sharing) and [_Fractal Rhythms_](https://soundcloud.com/underscore-opal/fractal-rythms?si=03c34741eacb4cfdbe0ff49bb1bd8c8f&utm_source=clipboard&utm_medium=text&utm_campaign=social_sharing) but they're a bit outdated now. They correspond aesthetically to what I aim for my album. They're composed out of very simple melodic and rythmical ideas that are deformed and extended as much as possible to create a whole song. I hope to make more music soon.
