### Note from \_opal

Hi. This is the very first bit of a project I wanted to make for quite a long time. I plan on releasing packs of assets as often as possible.
My goal is to share what I learn as an aspiring professional sound desinger. I want music and sound design to be accessible to anyone. Sharing stuff for free is one way to do so.

### Snares

This is a small sample pack inspired by the workflows of Mr. Bill and KOAN Sound. I made snares completely from scratch by layering some samples I recorded or synthesized. Each snare consists of a transient and at least one noise layer and one resonance layer. **Everything is made from scratch**.

### Content

- 7 clappy snares
- 2 fat snares
- 6 ghost snares
- 6 physmod snares
- 8 tight snares
- 2 tiny snares
- 2 very bad snares.
