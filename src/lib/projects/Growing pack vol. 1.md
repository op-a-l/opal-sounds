The growing pack is a project that has a lot of sense. I think I wouldn't have been making audio stuff nowadays if I didn't get access to the Mr. Bill's tutorials on YouTube. Same goes for many others like Alckemy, Lobotix or Polarity and many others. That is to say, free content is the key to make the world a little better by helping some folks to get access to art and culture. I don't think I am doing the best sh\*t out there but I hope my contribution can reveal one's curiosity.

That means you'll access some assets that represents the state of my learning. This will lead to more finished and large packs that will be sold. But you will decide the price so it is fair.
